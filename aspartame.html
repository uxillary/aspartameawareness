Aspartame Overview

Key facts about the popular sweetener.

Aspartame is a low-calorie artificial sweetener around 200 times sweeter than sugar. It is commonly found in diet sodas, sugar-free desserts, and other low-calorie products. While approved by many health agencies, aspartame continues to be scrutinized for potential links to headaches, mood changes, and other health effects.
